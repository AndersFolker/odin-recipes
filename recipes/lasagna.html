This is my Lasagna

It's an italian lasagna, and you can be sure it's the best pizza in the world.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta plates
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Minced meat
  • Onion

Steps:

  1. Cook minced meat with tomato sauce
  2. Add the cooked meat and sauce in a pan layered with pasta plates
  3. Cook for 30 minutes at 200 degrees celsius
