Salmon


This is my salmon dish

It's cooked salmon with vegetables, and you can be sure it's the best pizza in the world.

Ingredients:

  • Raw salmon files
  • Fresh vegetables
  • Olive Oil

Steps:

  1. Turn on oven to 200 degrees Celcius
  2. Add salmon to a pan with salt, pepper, sweet chili and lemon slices
  3. Put salmon in oven for 20 minutes
  4. Roast vegetables on a pan while salmon is in the oven
